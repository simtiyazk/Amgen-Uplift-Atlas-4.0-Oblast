Hello, Otezla Team!
We are excited to announce the rollout of the latest UPLIFT Atlas. This version includes novel UPLIFT data released in the new UPLIFT publication and at the 2021 ACR congress. These data provide key insights on disease and treatment burden in patients with PsO, as well as the impact of skin disease and depression in patients with PsA.
The Atlas is a global resource prepared for local teams. It is meant to serve as a comprehensive source of all available UPLIFT data organized into key UPLIFT takeaways. The aim of the Atlas is to ensure that you are aware of the rich insights from UPLIFT and to help facilitate tactical planning.
The updated resource includes:

A compendium of all available UPLIFT data in PsO and PsA, including insights from both patient and physician perspectives

An outline of projected UPLIFT data releases

Available regional subanalyses

Key comparisons with the 2012 MAPP
survey results
